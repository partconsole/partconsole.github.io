FX Power Gap Formula:
(Today's Rate − Past Rate) ÷ Past Rate × 100

What it means:
FX Power Gap measures how much the USD has strengthened or weakened compared to a past date.
- A positive gap means USD strengthened.
- A negative gap means USD weakened.
